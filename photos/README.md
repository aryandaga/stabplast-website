# Raw product photos — drop-off folder

Drop your real product photos here, named **exactly** as the filenames below
(each is the product's slug). A script normalizes them to the site's canonical
`1600×1200` 4:3 spec and writes them into `src/assets/images/products/`.

- Any of `.jpg` / `.jpeg` / `.png` is fine — just keep the basename exact.
- Prefer **landscape** shots; the crop is centre-weighted 4:3, so keep the
  subject roughly centered (portrait works but the sides get cropped).
